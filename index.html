Taiga Survival Guide

Objective

Survive in the taiga wilderness by managing health, warmth, hunger, and thirst. Avoid wolves and use resources to build and cook.

Core Mechanics

  • Movement: Use WASD to move. Stay warm and avoid wolves.
  • Interaction: Press E to gather resources or interact with structures.
  • Campfire: Build with 2 wood (1). Light with E. Melt snow to water or make tea.
  • Shelter: Build with 5 wood (2). Protects from wolves in green radius.
  • Stove: Build with 3 wood (3). Light with E. Cook meat (7), heat water (8), make tea (9).
  • Consumption: Use 4-6 for items. Cooked meat is healthier, hot water warms, tea boosts health.
  • Day/Night: Day: -5Â°C, Night: -20Â°C. Campfire provides warmth.
  • Wolves: Hungry predators. Hunt animals and player. Use shelter to avoid.

Tips

Gather wood first, build campfire for water, then stove for cooking. Monitor stats and avoid freezing!
